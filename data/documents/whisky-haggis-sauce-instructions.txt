How to Make Whisky Haggis Sauce - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Whisky Haggis Sauce:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Heat the frying pan or pot to medium.

Step 2:
Add the knob of butter and melt

Step 3:
Add 3 tbsp of your choice of whisky.

Step 4:
Light the whisky with a lighter and allow it to burn off the alcohol.

Step 5:
Add the cream, stock, and mustard.

Step 6:
Continue to stir and allow to thicken and reduce, stirring as necessary.

Step 7:
Add seasoning to taste.

Step 8:
If you would like a stronger whisky taste then you can add another tablespoon of whisky at the end too.
