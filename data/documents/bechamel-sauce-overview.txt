Béchamel Sauce Recipe - Classic White Sauce

This is a classic Béchamel sauce recipe. Béchamel is a white sauce made from a white roux (butter and flour) and milk. It has been enriched with cheese in this version.

KEY INFORMATION:
- Type: Sauce, Classic, Pasta
- Main ingredients: Butter, flour, milk, cheese, nutmeg
- Cuisine: French / Classic
- Flavor profile: Creamy, Savory, Cheesy
- Labels: Style - Pasta, Ingredient - Cheese, Style - Classic, Style - Sauce
