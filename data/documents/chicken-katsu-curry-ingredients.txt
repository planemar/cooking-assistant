Chicken Katsu Curry Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Chicken Katsu Curry:

MAIN INGREDIENTS:
- 260g basmati rice (90g per person)

CURRY SAUCE INGREDIENTS:
- 2 tbsp sunflower oil
- 2 onions, sliced
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- 3 carrots, sliced
- 2 tbsp plain flour
- 4 tsp curry powder
- 600ml chicken stock
- 1 tbsp honey
- 4 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tsp garam masala

CHICKEN INGREDIENTS:
- 4 chicken breasts (cover with cling film and beat flat with a rolling pin)
- 2 tbsp plain flour or cornflour
- 1 egg, beaten (optional added smoked paprika and chilli flakes in with the egg)
- 100g panko breadcrumbs
- 230ml vegetable oil for frying
- Seasoning, to taste
