How to Make Pesto Tagliatelle with Sea Bass - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Pesto Tagliatelle with Sea Bass:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Bring a pan of water to a boil for the pasta.

Step 2:
Add the tagliatelle and cook as per packet instructions (~11 minutes).

Step 3:
While the pasta is cooking, pan fry the seabass, cooking skin side down on a medium heat.

Step 4:
Before the pasta finishes cooking, add the butter to the pan.

Step 5:
Tilt the pan and spoon the melted butter over the seabass for a few minutes.

Step 6:
Add the tomatoes to the pan.

Step 7:
Drain the pasta.

Step 8:
Place the pasta back in the pan and mix in the cream and pesto.

Step 9:
Serve the pasta in a bowl with the seabass and tomatoes on top.
