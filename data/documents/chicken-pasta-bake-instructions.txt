How to Make Chicken Pasta Bake - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Chicken Pasta Bake:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Heat 2 tbsp of the oil in a pan over a medium heat and fry the onion gently for 10-12 mins.

Step 2:
Add the garlic and chilli flakes and cook for 1 min.

Step 3:
Tip in the tomatoes and sugar and season to taste.

Step 4:
Simmer uncovered for 20 mins or until thickened, then stir through the mascarpone.

Step 5:
Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a non-stick frying pan. Season the chicken and fry for 5-7 mins or until the chicken is cooked through.

Step 6:
Heat the oven to 220C/200C fan/gas 7.

Step 7:
Cook the penne following pack instructions. Drain and toss with the remaining oil.

Step 8:
Tip the pasta into the sauce along with the chicken and mix thoroughly before adding to the ovenproof dish.

Step 9:
Top with cheddar, mozzarella and parsley.

Step 10:
Bake for 20 mins or until golden brown and bubbling.
