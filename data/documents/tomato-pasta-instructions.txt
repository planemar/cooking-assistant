How to Make Tomato Pasta - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Tomato Pasta:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Cook off the garlic and onion in a pan with some olive oil on a medium heat.

Step 2:
Meanwhile cook the penne pasta.

Step 3:
Add the tomatoes, chilli and seasoning to the onions and bring to a slow simmer.

Step 4:
Once the pasta is ready, drain off and add to tomato sauce.

Step 5:
Top with parsley and some fresh ground pepper.
