How to Make Margherita Pizza - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Margherita Pizza:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Prepare your pizza base.

Step 2:
Top with the blitzed tomato sauce.

Step 3:
Add the mozzarella and grated Pecorino cheese.

Step 4:
Cook in a pizza oven or regular oven until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

Step 5:
Top with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil before serving.
