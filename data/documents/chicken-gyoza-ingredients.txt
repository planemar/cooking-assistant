Chicken Gyoza Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Chicken Gyoza:

WRAPPER INGREDIENTS:
- 270g plain flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 120ml hot water

FILLING INGREDIENTS:
- 110g Chinese leaf
- 150g cabbage
- 100g canned water chestnuts, drained
- 250g chicken thighs, skinless and boneless, minced
- 25g chives, finely chopped
- 25g cornflour
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp caster sugar
- Pinch of salt and white pepper
- Vegetable oil, for frying

SAUCE INGREDIENTS (makes 350ml):
- 1 large garlic clove, peeled and finely chopped
- 1 large red chilli, trimmed and finely chopped
- Salt
- 25g sugar
- 100ml malt vinegar
- 250ml light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
