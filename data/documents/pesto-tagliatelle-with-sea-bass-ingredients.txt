Pesto Tagliatelle with Sea Bass Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Pesto Tagliatelle with Sea Bass:

INGREDIENTS:
- 2 seabass fillets, butterflied
- 300g tagliatelle pasta
- 4 tbsp green pesto
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp cream
- Seasoning, to taste
- [Optional] Cherry tomatoes on the vine
