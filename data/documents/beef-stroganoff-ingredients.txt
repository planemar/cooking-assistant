Beef Stroganoff Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Beef Stroganoff:

INGREDIENTS:
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, sliced
- 1 garlic clove, crushed
- 1 tbsp butter
- 250g mushrooms, sliced
- 1 tbsp plain flour
- 500g fillet steak, sliced
- 150g crème fraîche
- 1 tsp English mustard
- 100ml beef stock
- Fresh parsley, chopped

SERVING:
- Pappardelle or rice
