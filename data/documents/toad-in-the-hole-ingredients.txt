Toad in the Hole Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Toad in the Hole:

INGREDIENTS:
- 6 best butchers sausages, I love using big Cumberland's
- 3 eggs
- 120g flour
- 120ml milk
- 1 tbsp mustard powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp dried rosemary
- 150ml beef gravy stock
- 2 large white onions
- Balsamic vinegar
- 100ml red wine
- Garden peas
- Fresh rosemary
- Cooking oil
