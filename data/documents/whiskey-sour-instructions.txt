How to Make Whiskey Sour - Step by Step Instructions

Complete instructions for making Whiskey Sour:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Mix all the ingredients except for the egg white in a shaker without ice.

Step 2:
Once mixed, add a few ice cubes and the egg white to the shaker and shaker again.

Step 3:
Pour into a cold glass using a strainer. Ensure some of the foamy goodness gets into the glass.
