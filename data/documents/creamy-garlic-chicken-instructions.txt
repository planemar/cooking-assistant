How to Make Creamy Garlic Chicken - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Creamy Garlic Chicken:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Use a rolling pin or meat mallet to flatten the chicken so they cook more evenly.

Step 2:
Season the chicken to taste and sprinkle with the garlic powder.

Step 3:
Dredge and coat the chicken with flour.

Step 4:
Warm the olive oil and 1/2 tbsp of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

Step 5:
Once the pan has warmed, sauté the chicken for 3-4 minutes per side.

Step 6:
When the chicken is golden brown remove the chicken and set aside on a plate.

Step 7:
Add the remaining butter to the pan.

Step 8:
Add the onion and cook for 3-4 minutes.

Step 9:
Crush 1/2 the garlic cloves with the side of a knife. Add the crushed cloves with the whole clovers to the pan.

Step 10:
Cook the garlic for 2-3 minutes, until light brown, careful not to burn the garlic..

Step 11:
Add the wine and bring to boil then simmer until reduced by half.

Step 12:
Now stir in the stock, lemon juice, and thyme and deglazing the pan as you go.

Step 13:
Let the sauce simmer for about 4 minutes. It should be noticeably reduced.

Step 14:
Pour in the cream. Bring back to a boil and simmer.

Step 15:
Add the chicken back in and continue to cook the chicken until cooked through.

Step 16:
Finish by adding the spinach and simmering for a further 1-2 minutes. Toss the spinach with the sauce until wilted.

Step 17:
Serve with the freshly chopped parsley.

NOTES:
- This dish is fine served on it's own but consider serving with a side of green beans, mash potato or sauté potatoes.
- Spaghetti also works well with this.
- You can switch out the chicken breast for boneless chicken thighs if you prefer.
