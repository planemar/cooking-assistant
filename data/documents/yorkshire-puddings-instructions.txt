How to Make Yorkshire Puddings - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Yorkshire Puddings:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Pre-heat the oven to 220Â°C.

Step 2:
Add some oil to the pudding tin to heat the oil.

Step 3:
Place the flour in a bowl, then make a well in the centre and break the egg into it.

Step 4:
Mix in half the milk using a wooden spoon, work the mixture until smooth then add the remaining milk.

Step 5:
Beat or whisk until fully combined and the surface is covered with tiny bubbles.

Step 6:
Allow to rest for 15 to 30 minutes, in the fridge, whisk again before cooking.

Step 7:
Pour the batter into the sizzling oil and bake for 10 to 15 minutes for individual puddings until risen and golden brown.
