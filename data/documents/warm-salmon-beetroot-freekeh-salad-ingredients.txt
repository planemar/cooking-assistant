Warm Salmon Beetroot Freekeh Salad Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Warm Salmon Beetroot Freekeh Salad:

INGREDIENTS:
- 250g raw beetroot, plus some beetroot stalks and leaves if you have them
- 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 40g Zaytoun smoky freekeh or plain freekeh
- 2 salmon fillets
- 50g rocket
- 50g creme fraiche
- 1 tbsp capers, finely chopped
- 1/2 lemon, juiced
- 1 tbsp water
