Employee Benefits

At TechVenture Inc., we offer a comprehensive benefits package designed to support our employees' health, well-being, and professional growth.

Health & Wellness:
- Premium health insurance with dental and vision coverage
- Mental health support through our Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Gym membership reimbursement up to $100/month
- Annual wellness stipend of $500

Time Off:
- 25 days of paid vacation per year
- 10 paid sick days annually
- 12 company holidays
- Parental leave: 16 weeks fully paid for primary caregivers, 8 weeks for secondary caregivers

Professional Development:
- Annual learning budget of $2,000 per employee
- Access to online learning platforms (Coursera, Udemy, LinkedIn Learning)
- Conference attendance support
- Internal mentorship program

Financial Benefits:
- Competitive salary packages
- 401(k) with 4% company match
- Stock options for all employees
- Annual performance bonuses

Work-Life Balance:
- Flexible work hours
- Remote work options (hybrid model)
- Home office setup stipend of $1,000
