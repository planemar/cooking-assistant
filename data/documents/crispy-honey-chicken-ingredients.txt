Crispy Honey Chicken Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Crispy Honey Chicken:

MAIN INGREDIENTS:
- 300g chicken thigh, diced
- 1 tsp sesame seeds, to serve
- 2 tbsp spring onions, chopped
- Onion, quartered
- Vegetable oil, for frying

BATTER INGREDIENTS:
- 90g corn flour
- 1/4 tsp Salt
- 1/4 tsp Baking Soda
- 4 egg whites
- 80ml cold water
- 155g mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp sweetened condensed milk

SAUCE INGREDIENTS:
- 110g honey
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine
