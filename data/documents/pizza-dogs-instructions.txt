How to Make Pizza Dogs - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Pizza Dogs:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Roll out pizza dough and wrap around hot dogs.

Step 2:
Top with tomato sauce, onions, mozzarella, or bacon as desired.

Step 3:
Bake until dough is golden brown and cooked through.

Step 4:
Serve with mustard and ketchup.
