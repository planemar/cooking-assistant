How to Make Chicken Katsu Curry - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Chicken Katsu Curry:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Cook the rice and drain once ready.

Step 2:
For the sauce. Heat oil in a medium saucepan and add in the chopped onion, garlic and spices until softened and golden brown.

Step 3:
Add the carrots on low heat for 10 minutes.

Step 4:
Add flour cook for 1 minute.

Step 5:
Gradually pour in the stock until combined and then add honey, soy sauce and bay leaf.

Step 6:
Slowly bring to the boil.

Step 7:
Turn down the heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until sauce thickens but is still of pouring consistency.

Step 8:
Leave to cool slightly, then blend the mixture until smooth (use a sieve if you don't have a hand blender). Keep warm.

Step 9:
For the chicken, add the flour, beaten eggs and panko breadcrumbs into three bowls (place in a line leading to the frying pan.

Step 10:
Heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium-high heat.

Step 11:
Seat the chicken breasts before covering in flour, then the egg and finally the breadcrumbs.

Step 12:
Fry the chicken breasts for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.

Step 13:
Remove from the heat and use kitchen tissue paper to remove any excess oil.

Step 14:
Use a small bowl or mould to serve the rice. Then add a chicken breast and a nice covering of curry sauce.
