Prawn Pad Thai Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Prawn Pad Thai:

MAIN INGREDIENTS:
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 200g king prawns, shelled, deveined and heads removed if preferred
- 1 tbsp sweet radish, or tamarind
- 150g handful bean sprouts
- 3 spring onions, chopped
- 1 red chilli, finely chopped
- 300g rice noodles, soaked in warm water for 8-10 minutes, then drained and dried

SAUCE INGREDIENTS:
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 3 tbsp sugar
- drizzle sesame oil
- 1 tsp rice vinegar
- 2 tbsp water

TO SERVE:
- Chilli powder, to taste
- 4 tbsp roasted peanuts, crushed
- 1 tbsp, sugar
- Lime quarter
