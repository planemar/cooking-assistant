How to Make Béchamel Sauce - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Béchamel sauce:

COOKING METHOD:

Step 1:
Melt the butter in a pan.

Step 2:
Add the flour and stir until you had a dough.

Step 3:
Gradually add the milk stirring with a whisk.

Step 4:
Keep adding the milk and stirring until you have a nice thick sauce without any lumps.

Step 5:
Stir in the cheese.

Step 6:
Add the seasoning and pinch of nutmeg.
