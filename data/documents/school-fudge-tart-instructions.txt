How to Make School Fudge Tart - Step by Step Instructions

Complete baking instructions for making School Fudge Tart:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Makeup pastry as normal – then line and grease 8″ flan tin.

Step 2:
Bake blind for 15 minutes at 180ºC.

Step 3:
Pour half the milk into a saucepan, add butter and heat till nearly boiling.

Step 4:
While you wait, blend the remainder of milk with the flour and beat to a smooth paste.

Step 5:
Add the flour/milk paste to the hot milk mixture, quickly add the sugar and cook thoroughly (stir and stir and stir) until a thick smooth finish is obtained.

Step 6:
Add the vanilla essence and beat through.

Step 7:
Pour in the pastry case and leave to cool.

Step 8:
Decorate with the grated chocolate.
