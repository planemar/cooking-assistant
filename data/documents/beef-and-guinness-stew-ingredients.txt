Beef and Guinness Stew Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Beef and Guinness Stew:

INGREDIENTS:
- 500g beef, diced
- 1 can of Guinness
- 2 onions, chopped
- 2 garlic, crushed
- 60g tomato paste
- 4 sprigs of thyme
- 3 carrots, chopped
- 3 celery stalks, chopped
- 500ml chicken stock
- 200g baby potatoes
- Seasoning, to taste

SERVING:
- Ciabatta bread
