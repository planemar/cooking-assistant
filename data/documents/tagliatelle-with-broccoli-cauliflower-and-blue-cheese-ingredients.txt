Tagliatelle with Broccoli, Cauliflower and Blue Cheese Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Tagliatelle with Broccoli, Cauliflower and Blue Cheese:

INGREDIENTS:
- 170g spinach tagliatelle pasta
- 150g broccoli florets
- 150g cauliflower florets
- 200g blue cheese (Cambazola)
- Nutmeg, freshly grated
- Seasoning, to taste
