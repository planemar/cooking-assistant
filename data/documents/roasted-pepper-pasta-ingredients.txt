Roasted Pepper Pasta Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Roasted Pepper Pasta:

INGREDIENTS:
- 400g rigatoni pasta
- 3 red peppers
- 1 red onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 50g cashews
- 1 tbsp dried oregano
- 1 tbsp dried basil
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 200ml milk or cream
- Seasoning to taste
