Bosnian Stuffed Peppers (Punjene Paprike) Recipe

Punjena paprika is a traditional dish consisting of peppers stuffed with a mix of meat and rice in tomato sauce.

KEY INFORMATION:
- Type: Main
- Main ingredients: Beef, Peppers, Rice
- Serves: 8
- Prep Time: 15 minutes
- Cook Time: 45 minutes
- Total Time: 1 hour
- Cuisine: Bosnian / Balkan
