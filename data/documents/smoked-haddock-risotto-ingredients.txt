Smoked Haddock Risotto Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Smoked Haddock Risotto:

INGREDIENTS:
- 700ml chicken stock
- 200g smoked haddock
- 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 4 tbsp butter
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 3 flatleaf parsley stems, stalks finely chopped, leaves reserved and roughly chopped
- 180g risotto rice
- 120ml white wine
- 1 lemon, zest only
- Eggs, one per person
- Chives, chopped finely, for garnish
- Seasoning, to taste
