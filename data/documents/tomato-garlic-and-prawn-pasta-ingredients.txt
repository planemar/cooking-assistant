Tomato, Garlic and Prawn Pasta Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Tomato, Garlic and Prawn Pasta:

INGREDIENTS:
- Spaghetti
- King prawns, peeled and deveined, 4-5 per person, halved
- 1 cans of plum tomatoes
- 1 tbsp tomato puree
- 1/2 onion, finely chopped
- 4 garlic cloves, crushed
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Splash of white wine
- Pinch of fennel seeds, crushed
- Olive oil
- Fresh parsley, finely chopped
- Seasoning to taste
