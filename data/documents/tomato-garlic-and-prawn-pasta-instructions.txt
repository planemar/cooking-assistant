How to Make Tomato, Garlic and Prawn Pasta - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Tomato, Garlic and Prawn Pasta:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Cook off the onions and two crushed garlic cloves in a pan with some olive oil.

Step 2:
Add the tomatoes and simmer for 20-30 minutes, seasoning to taste.

Step 3:
In a separate pan toss the prawns in some butter with the final two crushed garlic cloves.

Step 4:
Cook the pasta in salted water until cooked.

Step 5:
Before draining set aside a ladle or two of the water used to cook the pasta in a bowl.

Step 6:
Add the cooked pasta to the pan of pasta sauce.

Step 7:
Loosen the pasta with the pasta cooking water if needed.

Step 8:
Add the prawns and stir to mix together.

Step 9:
Serve with a topping of fresh pasta and grating of parmesan cheese.
