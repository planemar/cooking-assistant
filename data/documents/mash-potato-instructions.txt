How to Make Mash Potato - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Mash Potato:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Place chopped potatoes in a medium pot, add water and cook on high heat on the stove. When they start boiling, cook for 5 minutes.

Step 2:
Reduce heat to medium-low and cook for another 5 minutes.

Step 3:
Remove pot from the stove and discard the water. Add butter, milk, salt and black pepper and the potatoes back into the pan and use a potato masher.

Step 4:
Gradually add more milk and continue to mash until you have creamy and buttery texture.

Step 5:
Serve warm.
