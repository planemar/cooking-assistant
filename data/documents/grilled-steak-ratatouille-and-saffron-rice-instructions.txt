How to Make Grilled Steak Ratatouille and Saffron Rice - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Grilled Steak Ratatouille and Saffron Rice:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Cut the courgette and aubergine and place in a hot griddle pan.

Step 2:
Place the rice ingredients in a small pan of boiling water.

Step 3:
Add the peppers and onions to the casserole pan with the harissa and anchovies and the squashed garlic.

Step 4:
Once cooked, add the courgette and aubergine together with the passata and balsamic vinegar.

Step 5:
Rub the steaks with salt and paprika and cook to your liking.

Step 6:
Finely chop the parsley, add the mustard, oil, seasoning, and lemon juice and mix together on a board ready for the steak. Once the steak is ready, toss together with these ingredients.

Step 7:
Tear the basil into the ratatouille.

Step 8:
Serve with yoghurt and saffron rice.
