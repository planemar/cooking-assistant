Chicken Balmoral Recipe

Chicken Balmoral is a Scottish dish of chicken breast stuffed with haggis and wrapped in bacon.

KEY INFORMATION:
- Type: Main
- Main ingredients: Chicken breast, Haggis, Bacon
- Serves: 2
- Prep Time: 10 minutes
- Cook Time: 50 minutes
- Total Time: 1 hour
- Labels: Ingredient - Meat - Lamb, Ingredient - Meat - Chicken, Style - Scottish
