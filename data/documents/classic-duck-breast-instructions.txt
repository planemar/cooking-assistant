How to Make Classic Duck Breast - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Classic Duck Breast:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Preheat the oven to 180°C.

Step 2:
Crosshatch score the skin and fat. Don't score so deep you cut the meat.

Step 3:
Season both sides of the duck.

Step 4:
Place skin down into a cold pan and bring the pan up to medium to low heat (6-8 minutes). This renders out the fat.

Step 5:
As the duck cooks, tilt the pan and remove any excess oil for a more consistent cook. If you're cooking mash, add the oil t the potato once mashed.

Step 6:
Turn the fillets and cook the other side for about a 1 minute.

Step 7:
Place skin down and continue cook in the oven (~4 minutes) until the internal temperature of the duck reads 52°C (medium-rare).

Step 8:
Remove the duck from the oven and allow to rest (~5 minutes).

Step 9:
Cut the duck breast at an angle and plate over hot mash and a side of creamed greens.

NOTES:
- Serve with creamed greens and a buttery mash or alternatively asparagus when in season and a red wine jus.
