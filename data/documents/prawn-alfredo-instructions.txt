How to Make Prawn Alfredo - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Prawn Alfredo:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Add the pasta to a pan of boiling water and cook until al dente.

Step 2:
Mix the prawns with some olive oil and seasoning to taste.

Step 3:
Melt some butter in a warming pan.

Step 4:
While the pasta is cooking saut√© the shallots and grated garlic until soft and golden.

Step 5:
Add the prawns to the hot pan until they are just cooked through.

Step 6:
If you want to add some heat, add the chilli flakes or paprika.

Step 7:
Remove the pasta and drain but keep the starch water back.

Step 8:
Add the white wine and a splash of lemon juice to the hot pan and reduce.

Step 9:
Once the wine has reduced add the cream and simmer for 2-3 minutes.

Step 10:
Add a few spoons of starch water to thicken.

Step 11:
Add the parmesan cheese and nutmeg.

Step 12:
Mix the cooked pasta and prawns until covered in the creamy alfredo sauce.

Step 13:
Serve topped with some fresh parmesan and fresh parsley.
