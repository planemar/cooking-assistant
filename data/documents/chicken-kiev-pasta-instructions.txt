How to Make Chicken Kiev Pasta - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Chicken Kiev Pasta:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Bake the chicken in the oven as per the instructions.

Step 2:
While the chicken is cooking fry off the crushed garlic with some butter and olive oil.

Step 3:
After a few minutes add the chopped parsley.

Step 4:
Cook the spaghetti.

Step 5:
Once the pasta is ready, add it to the garlic butter using tongs or a spaghetti spoon.

Step 6:
Loosen the garlic butter with some of the pasta water.

Step 7:
Mix the spaghetti thoroughly with the garlic butter.

Step 8:
Season to taste and add a pinch of nutmeg.

Step 9:
Serve the pasta topped with the Chicken Kiev or Chicken Schnitzel and parmesan.
