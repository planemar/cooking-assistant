How to Make Chicken Schnitzel - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Chicken Schnitzel:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Butterfly fillet the chicken breasts.

Step 2:
Cover the chicken breasts with cling film and carefully hit them with a rolling pin so they have a consistent thickness.

Step 3:
Place each breast in a bowl of flour, ensuring the breast is full covered in flour.

Step 4:
After covering in flour, repeat the process but this time in a bowl of whisked eggs.

Step 5:
Finish by placing each breast in a bowl of panko breadcrumbs. Push the breadcrumbs down onto each chicken breast ensuring they are nicely covered in breadcrumbs.

Step 6:
Place the breadcrumb covered chicken breasts on a tray and place them in the fridge for 30 minutes.

Step 7:
Heat oil in a deep pan or warm oil in a fryer.

Step 8:
Remove the chicken breasts from the fridge and fry for 2-3 minutes on each side.

NOTES:
- Serving suggestions mash potato, crispy fried onions or fries.
