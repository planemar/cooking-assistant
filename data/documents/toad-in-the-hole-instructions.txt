How to Make Toad in the Hole - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Toad in the Hole:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Preheat the over to 225Â°C.

Step 2:
Brown off the sausages in a frying pan.

Step 3:
While the sausages are browning, add 3-4 tbsp of oil to a casserole dish and add to the oven to get it smoking hot.

Step 4:
Prepare the batter by mixing the eggs and flour together.

Step 5:
Add the milk, mustard powder, rosemary, paprika and seasoning to taste.

Step 6:
Remove the casserole dish from the oven (ensure it goes on a heat proof surface).

Step 7:
Quickly place the sausages into the casserole dish.

Step 8:
Pour the batter around the sizzling hot tray and topping with rosemary.

Step 9:
Return back to the oven for 25 minutes.

Step 10:
The trick for a crispy batter is to not open the oven until the batter is cooked.

Step 11:
Finely chop the onion and add to a pan with some oil or butter to sweat off.

Step 12:
Keep stirring them to prevent them from burning.

Step 13:
Once the onions have caramelised, add some flour and mix around and then reduce the red wine in a pan.

Step 14:
Add the gravy and bring to a simmer.

Step 15:
Mix in a few splashes of balsamic vinegar to the gravy.

Step 16:
Ahead of serving bring water in a pan to a boil to cook the peas.

Step 17:
Serve with gravy and peas.
