Cinnamon Swirls Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Cinnamon Swirls:

CINNAMON SWIRLS INGREDIENTS:
- 50 g Butter or Margarine (at room temperature)
- 50 g Demerara Sugar
- 1 tbsp Ground Cinnamon
- 1 Pack of Ready Roll Puff Pastry (375g)
- 1 Free-range Egg (beaten)
- Extra Sugar for Sprinkling

ICING INGREDIENTS:
- 2 tbsp Icing Sugar
- Few drops of Warm Water
