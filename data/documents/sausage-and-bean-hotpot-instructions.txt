How to Make Sausage and Bean Hotpot - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Sausage and Bean Hotpot:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Pre-heat the oven to 180°C.

Step 2:
For the ragù, cook off the fresh chillies, garlic cloves and onions in a oven proof casserole dish to give some colour before adding some olive oil.

Step 3:
Add the tomato puree and cook through until the onions are soft.

Step 4:
[Optional] If you're going for the hot hotpot variant, add the chorizo, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper and cook for a few minutes.

Step 5:
Mix in the thyme, rosemary and chicken stock.

Step 6:
Stir in the BBQ sauce and add the cherry tomatoes.

Step 7:
Add the pearl barley, kidney beans, butter beans and the can tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 8:
Add the sausages (either whole or chopped, I prefer whole) and cook in the oven for 45 mins at 180°C.

Step 9:
[Optional] Warm the sough dough in the oven ahead of serving.

Step 10:
Remove the dish from the oven and deglaze the pan with some water if necessary.

Step 11:
Before serving, add a few drops of balsamic vinegar.

Step 12:
Serve hot with the warm sough dough.
