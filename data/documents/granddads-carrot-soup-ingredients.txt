Granddads Carrot Soup Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Granddads Carrot Soup:

INGREDIENTS:
- 3 tbsp butter
- 450g carrots, peeled and grated
- 220g potatoes, peeled and diced
- 2 onions, finely chopped
- 850ml vegetable stock
- 2 tsp white pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tsp of Paprika
- 2 tbsp double cream
- 1 tbsp of fennel seeds
