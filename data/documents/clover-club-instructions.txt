How to Make Clover Club - Step by Step Instructions

Complete instructions for making Clover Club:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Add all the ingredients, except for the garnish, to a cocktail shaker and dry shake (without ice).

Step 2:
Then add ice and shake for a second time.

Step 3:
Strain into a chilled cocktail coupe glass.

Step 4:
Spear your raspberries on a cocktail pick and perch over the kip of the glass.
