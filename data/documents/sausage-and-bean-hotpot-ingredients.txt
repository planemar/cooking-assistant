Sausage and Bean Hotpot Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Sausage and Bean Hotpot:

INGREDIENTS:
- Sausages (2-3 Cumberland sausages per serving)
- 2 tbsp tomato puree
- 2 red chilli, finely chopped
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 1 chorizo, diced into lardon like cubes
- 2 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp rosemary
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1 crushed chicken stock cube
- 100g pearl barley
- 50ml BBQ sauce
- 1 can of butter beans
- 1 can of kidney beans
- 1 handful cherry tomatoes
- 2 bay leaves
- Olive oil
