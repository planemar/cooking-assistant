How to Make Carrot Cake - Step by Step Instructions

Complete baking instructions for making Carrot Cake:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Heat the oven to 180Â°C.

Step 2:
Grease 2 x 20cm sandwich tins.

Step 3:
Beat the butter, eggs and sugar until fluffy.

Step 4:
Fold in the remaining ingredients along with the milk to loosen.

Step 5:
Divide the mix between the two tins.

Step 6:
Bake for 35-45 minutes until a skewer comes out clean.

Step 7:
Cool in the tins briefly, then on a cooling rack.

FROSTING METHOD:

Step 8:
Beat the butter and icing sugar with the lemon zest.

Step 9:
Add the cream cheese and continue to beat.

Step 10:
Place icing on the top of one cakes before placing the cake on top to form a cake and icing sandwich.

Step 11:
Top the cake with the remainder of the icing.

Step 12:
Decorate the icing with crushed/chopped walnuts and pecans around the edge in a circular fashion.

NOTES:
