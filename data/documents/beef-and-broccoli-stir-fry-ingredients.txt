Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry:

MARINADE INGREDIENTS:
- 1 tsp cornflour
- 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp vegetable oil
- 2 Sirloin steak, trimmed of fat and sliced thin against the grain

SAUCE INGREDIENTS:
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 110ml oyster sauce
- 110ml chicken stock
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp light brown sugar
- 2 tsp cornflour

STIR FRY INGREDIENTS:
- 3 tbsp vegetable oil
- 3 onions, cut into wedges
- 300g broccoli florets
- 1 medium carrot, peeled and cut into match sticks
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 1/4 tsp crushed chillies
- Spring onions, sliced, for garnish
- 1 tsp sesame seeds, toasted, for garnish
- Basmati rice
