How to Make Creamy Smoked Salmon Pasta - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Creamy Smoked Salmon Pasta:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Cook the pasta as per the packets instructions.

Step 2:
Cook the salmon fillets skin down until crispy.

Step 3:
In the same pan fry of the shallot and garlic.

Step 4:
Add two teaspoons of mustard.

Step 5:
Mix in the cream and flake up the salmon.

Step 6:
Use a spaghetti spatula to drain the pasta before gradually adding some of the water from the pan to the salmon.

Step 7:
Mix the pasta with the salmon and sauce.

Step 8:
If it looks a little dry add some more of the pasta water.

Step 9:
Serve with freshly chopped parsley and a sprinkle of sea salt.
