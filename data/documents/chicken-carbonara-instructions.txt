How to Make Chicken Carbonara - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Chicken Carbonara:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Fry off the bacon or pancetta with the garlic and set aside discarding the garlic clove.

Step 2:
Fry off the chicken and set them aside.

Step 3:
Cook the spaghetti and drain (keep a ladle of the pasta water for the sauce).

Step 4:
Beat two eggs, and two yolks (add a tiny pinch of nutmeg).

Step 5:
Combine the chicken and grated parmesan in a pan off the heat.

Step 6:
Add the egg to the pan and then add the spaghetti slowly mixing until creamy.

Step 7:
Add a tablespoon of pasta water to loosen things up if needed.

Step 8:
Season well with some cracked black pepper.

Step 9:
Serve with the pancetta (for those that want it) and top with a few cuttings of parmesan.

NOTES:
- Try wilting some spinach when cooking the chicken.
