School Fudge Tart Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making School Fudge Tart:

PASTRY INGREDIENTS:
- 170g plain flour
- 85g butter
- (original recipe states 1 1/2oz marg, 1 1/2oz lard/ shortening)
- 45ml water

FILLING INGREDIENTS:
- 285ml milk
- 85g sugar
- 115g butter
- 60g plain flour
- 1 tsp vanilla essence
- 15g chocolate, grated
