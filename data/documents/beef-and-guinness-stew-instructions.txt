How to Make Beef and Guinness Stew - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Beef and Guinness Stew:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Brown off the beef in a casserole dish.

Step 2:
Add the onions and celery and allow to soften.

Step 3:
Pour in the Guinness.

Step 4:
Add the garlic, herbs, seasoning tomato paste and chicken stock.

Step 5:
Bring to a boil before simmering for 40 minutes.

Step 6:
Add the carrots and potatoes and cook for a further 20 minutes.

NOTES:
Serve with a Ciabatta bread warmed in the oven.
