How to Make Strawberry and Banana Dessert Pizza with a Cookie Base - Step by Step Instructions

Complete baking instructions for making Strawberry and Banana Dessert Pizza with a Cookie Base:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Preheat the pizza oven to 400Â°C.

Step 2:
Roll out the dough and add a piece of chocolate around the outside, then fold the dough over to form a chocolate stuffed crust.

Step 3:
Melt 1-2tbsp of cookie butter or biscuit spread in a bowl in the microwave for 30 seconds or enough to make it soft to spread over the pizza dough.

Step 4:
Once melted, spread the cookie dough over the pizza base.

Step 5:
Slice the banana and strawberries as desired and place across the pizza evenly.

Step 6:
Top with sprinkles of crushed biscuits.

Step 7:
Turn off the flame in the pizza oven ahead of launching.

Step 8:
Allow the base to cook slowly before turning the flame back on to bake the top and crust.
