How to Make Cauliflower Cheese - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Cauliflower Cheese:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Cut cauliflower into small florets (use the roots too) and parboil.

Step 2:
Make Béchamel sauce as per instructions.

Step 3:
Add some grated cheese to the sauce.

Step 4:
Place the drained cauliflower on a baking tray and pour the Béchamel sauce over the cauliflower.

Step 5:
Sprinkle some cheese over the top and place in the oven/grill for 20 minutes.
