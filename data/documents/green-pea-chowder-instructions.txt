How to Make Green Pea Chowder - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Green Pea Chowder:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Heat up the butter in a large pan over medium heat.

Step 2:
Simmer all the ingredients apart from the cream for 15 minutes.

Step 3:
Remove the bay leaves and liquidise with a hand blender.

Step 4:
Thin out with cream and add seasoning as necessary.

Step 5:
Garnish servings with a chive sprig and a decorative drizzle of whipping cream.
