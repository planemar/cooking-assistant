How to Make Steak Pinwheels - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Steak Pinwheels:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Tenderise and flatten the steak.

Step 2:
If adding bacon, create a layer of bacon over the steak.

Step 3:
Season and spread the crushed on both sides of the steak.

Step 4:
Layer the spinach over the steak.

Step 5:
If adding cheese, crumble the blue cheese over the across the spinach.

Step 6:
Roll the steak and secure with toothpicks.

Step 7:
Cut into 6 pinwheel slices.

Step 8:
Heat a cast iron on medium to high heat.

Step 9:
Add olive oil to the pan and sear the pinwheels for 2-3 minutes on each side.

Step 10:
Place skillet with the steaks into the oven and cook for 7-10 minutes.

Step 11:
Remove the toothpicks.

Step 12:
Serve pinwheels with mash or rice and greens.
