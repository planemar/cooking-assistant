Sticky Salmon with Broccoli Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Sticky Salmon with Broccoli:

INGREDIENTS:
- 2 heads of broccoli, broken into florets, sliced in half
- 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
- 1 tbsp dark soy
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tsp garlic paste
- 1 tsp ginger paste
- Salmon fillets
- Spring onions, finely sliced, to serve
- Serve with hot or cold rice
