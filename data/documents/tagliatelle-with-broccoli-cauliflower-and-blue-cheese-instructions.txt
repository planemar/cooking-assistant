How to Make Tagliatelle with Broccoli, Cauliflower and Blue Cheese - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Tagliatelle with Broccoli, Cauliflower and Blue Cheese:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Cook the pasta (10-12 minutes), until al dente.

Step 2:
While the pasta cooks trim the broccoli and cauliflower florets into bite-sized pieces.

Step 3:
Add the florets to the pasta for the last 2-3 minutes.

Step 4:
Drain the pasta and vegetables into a colander.

Step 5:
Return the dry pan to the hob on a low heat and add the blue cheese.

Step 6:
Let the cheese melt, stirring frequently to make a smooth sauce.

Step 7:
Once the cheese has melted add the cooked pasta and vegetables.

Step 8:
Turn the pasta and mix well and heat through.

Step 9:
Season to taste with nutmeg, salt and black pepper.
