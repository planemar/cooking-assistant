Mums Delicious Chocolate Brownies Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Mums Delicious Chocolate Brownies:

INGREDIENTS:
- 140g unsalted butter softened plus a small amount for lining the baking tin
- 200g quality plain chocolate such as Lindt or Hotel Chocolat
- 200g caster sugar
- Pinch of sea salt
- 2 tsp vanilla extract / vanilla paste
- 2 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 85g plain flour
