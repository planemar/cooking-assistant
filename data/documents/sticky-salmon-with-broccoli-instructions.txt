How to Make Sticky Salmon with Broccoli - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Sticky Salmon with Broccoli:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.

Step 2:
Place the broccoli florets into a baking dish, drizzle over the sesame oil and toss the broccoli around to coat it in the oil. Pop into the oven and bake for 5 minutes.

Step 3:
Meanwhile, make up the sauce in a small bowl by mixing together the soy sauce, honey, garlic and ginger.

Step 4:
After 5 minutes, remove the broccoli from the oven and give it a good stir around in the dish. Place the salmon fillets, skin side down, in the baking dish and spoon the sauce over each fillet, drizzling any remaining sauce over the broccoli.

Step 5:
Place in the oven and cook for 12 minutes.

Step 6:
Serve, removing the skin from the salmon fillets, if you like, then scattering with the spring onions.
