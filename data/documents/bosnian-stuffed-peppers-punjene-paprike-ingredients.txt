Bosnian Stuffed Peppers Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making Bosnian Stuffed Peppers (Punjene Paprike):

MAIN INGREDIENTS:
- 8-10 white peppers
- 600g ground beef
- 80g rice
- 1 large potato, finely chopped
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 2-3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 1 tbsp flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp tomato puree
- 1 tsp Vegeta spice
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

SAUCE INGREDIENTS:
- 2-3 tbsp cooking oil
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1 tsp ground paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- 120ml tomato puree
- 1l water
