How to Make Mums Delicious Chocolate Brownies - Step by Step Instructions

Complete baking instructions for making Mums Delicious Chocolate Brownies:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Preheat oven to 180°C.

Step 2:
Line a brownie tin with a small amount of butter (7″ square tin is most suitable)

Step 3:
Melt the butter and chocolate on a slow heat and then allow to cool slightly.

Step 4:
Mix in the sugar, a pinch of sea salt and the vanilla extract, stopping once the mixture is a little grainy.

Step 5:
Whisk in the eggs and yolk one at a time, stirring each time until blended.

Step 6:
Sift in the flour and beat for a minute or so until thickened and smooth.

Step 7:
Pour the mixture into the tin and bake for 40-45 minutes or until a cocktail stick comes out with moist crumbs.

Step 8:
Allow to cool and cut into small rectangles slices.
