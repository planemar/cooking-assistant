How to Make Roasted Pepper Pasta - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Roasted Pepper Pasta:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Firstly place the peppers + onion + garlic into a baking dish and roast for 30 minutes at 220C (430F).

Step 2:
Remove from the oven and allow to cool for 15 minutes.

Step 3:
Meanwhile soak your cashews in boiling water (helps to soften them) and boil your pasta.

Step 4:
Once the peppers have cooled peel off the skin, remove the core and discard. Place all into a blender along with the drained cashews + oregano + nutritional yeast + salt & pepper + milk and blend until smooth.

Step 5:
Drain the pasta placing back into the pot, pour over the sauce and mix it through.
