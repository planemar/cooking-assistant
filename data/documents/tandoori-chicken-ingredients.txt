Tandoori Chicken Ingredients List

Complete ingredient list for making authentic tandoori chicken (serves 2-3):

MAIN INGREDIENTS:
- 2-3 boneless skinless chicken breasts
- 4-5 boneless skinless chicken thighs
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 1 tsp sea salt
- 2 onions, quartered and layers separated
- Vegetable oil for brushing

MARINADE INGREDIENTS (the key to authentic tandoori chicken):
The marinade is what makes tandoori chicken special - the yogurt tenderizes while the spices create the classic flavor.

- 300ml Greek yoghurt (tenderizes the chicken)
- Large piece ginger, grated
- 4 garlic cloves, crushed
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 lemon or lime
- 1 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp extra hot chilli powder
- 2 tbsp tandoori masala powder (essential for tandoori flavor - this is the key spice)
- 1 tbsp cumin powder
- 1 tbsp coriander powder
- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1 tsp ground pepper
- 1 tsp citrus powder
- Fresh coriander leaves, roughly chopped
- Red food colouring powder (optional for appearance)

SPICE NOTES:
Tandoori masala is the most important ingredient - it contains a blend of spices including cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and cayenne that create the signature tandoori flavor.
