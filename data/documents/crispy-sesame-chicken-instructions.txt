How to Make Crispy Sesame Chicken - Step by Step Instructions

Complete cooking instructions for making Crispy Sesame Chicken:

METHOD:

Step 1:
Combine all the sauce ingredients and mix thoroughly.

Step 2:
Heat the oil to 180Â°C in a suitable pan or deep fryer.

Step 3:
Prepare the batter before coating the chicken pieces in the batter.
